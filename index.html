Welcome to play a game of ROCK, PAPER, SCISSORS.

In this game, you get to choose a weapon (Rock, paper or scissors)
You choose by clicking on the red icons in the players choice area.
The computer will generate its choice of weapon for a counter attack.

Rock beats Scissors
Scissors beat Paper
Paper beats Rock

The one of you with the strongest weapon wins the round.
This will generate a point in the score area.

The one who gets to three points first, wins the game.

Good luck!
